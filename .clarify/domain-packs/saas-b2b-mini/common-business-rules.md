# Common Business Rules: B2B SaaS

Candidate rules — present as ASSUMPTION / OPEN QUESTION until confirmed.

- An org must always have at least one Owner.
- Members cannot escalate their own role.
- Seat count enforced by the plan; invites blocked when seats are full.
- Data is isolated per tenant; cross-tenant access is never allowed.
- Role changes take effect immediately (or next session?) — confirm.
- Deactivating a user revokes access but retains their audit history.
- Invitation expiry (e.g. 7 days) — confirm value.
