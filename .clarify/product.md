# Clarify — Product Definition

## What Clarify is

An **agent-native requirement-quality skill pack**. It improves and scores
software requirements so that gaps, ambiguity, and missing edge cases are caught
before they reach Dev and QA.

## What Clarify is NOT

- NOT a PRD generator. It does not fabricate a product on your behalf.
- NOT a domain expert that invents business rules. Unknown rules are surfaced as
  `ASSUMPTION` or `OPEN QUESTION`.
- NOT a code generator, web app, or CLI binary (those are out of MVP 1 scope).

## Who it serves

- **Product managers / BAs** turning rough ideas or weak PRDs into build-ready
  specs.
- **Engineers** who want unambiguous, testable, traceable requirements.
- **QA** who need acceptance criteria and test scenarios derived from the spec.

## The core loop

1. **Intake** — a raw idea (`from-idea`); or score an existing doc (`audit`).
2. **Quality analysis** — detect anti-patterns, score against the rubric.
3. **Improvement** — resolve the Answer Sheet and shape, clarify, add user stories,
   edge cases, and testable acceptance criteria (`improve`).
4. **Finalize** — compile the confirmed spec into the sign-off **URD** for BA/PO
   sign-off (`finalize`), then render HTML/Word (`export`).

## Value proposition

Requirement defects are the cheapest to fix and the most expensive to miss.
Clarify makes requirement quality **measurable** (a reproducible score) and
**actionable** (specific anti-patterns mapped to specific fixes), without
locking teams into a single domain or tool.
